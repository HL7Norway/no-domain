Resource Profile: NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Oxygen Saturation

Official URL: http://hl7.no/fhir/StructureDefinition/no-domain-vso-oxygensaturation Version: 0.0.1-alpha
Draft as of 2021-05-01 Computable Name: NoDomainVSOOxygenSaturation

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Oxygen Saturation information.
Use to record blood oxygen and related measurements, measured by pulse oximetry or pulse CO-oximetry.

Basisprofile for Norwegian VitalSigns Observation Oxygen Saturation information. Defined by The Norwegian Directorate of eHealth and HL7 Norway. The profile adds Norwegian specific property information and further explanation of the use for the data-elements in a Norwegian VitalSigns Observation Oxygen Saturation.

Usage:

  • This Resource Profile is not used by any profiles in this Implementation Guide
