Structures: Resource Profiles

These define constraints on FHIR resources for systems conforming to this implementation guide

NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Blood pressure

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Blood pressure information. The local measurement of arterial blood pressure, which is a surrogate for arterial pressure in the systemic circulation.
NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Body Height

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Body Height information.
To be used for recording the measured height or body length of an individual at any point in time.
NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Body Mass Index (BMI)

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Body Mass Index information. Calculated measurement which compares a person’s weight and height.
NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Body Temperature

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Body Temperature information. Used for recording the measurement of an individual’s body temperature, which is a surrogate for the core body temperature of the individual.
NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Body Weight

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Body Weight information. To be used for recording the actual measurement of body weight, including when the individual is missing a body part due to a congenital cause or after surgical removal.
NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Heart Rate

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Heart Rate information.
Use to record the presence or absence of a pulse or heart beat.
NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Oxygen Saturation

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Oxygen Saturation information.
Use to record blood oxygen and related measurements, measured by pulse oximetry or pulse CO-oximetry.
NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Pulse

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Pulse information. The rate and associated attributes for a pulse.
NO Domain VitalSigns Observation - Respiration Rate

Base profile for Norwegian Vital Signs Observation Respiration Rate information.
Use to record the observed and measured characteristics of spontaneous breathing by an individual, including respiratory rate, depth and rhythm.
Vkp VitalSigns Observation - Blood pressure

Profile for blood pressure in VKP.
