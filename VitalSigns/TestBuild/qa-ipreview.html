    web arketyper.no The profiles includes SNOMED CT terms for each vital sign measurement. The SNOMED terms are chosen to best reflect the concept expressed in the corresponding LOINC magic value for each profile. When choosing a SNOMED term both Norwegian clinical practice have been taken into account (example: what does the clinicians usually understand when reading a heart rate observation) in most cases this maps directly to the concept described in the corresponding OpenEHR Archetype . In one case this has led to a separate profile (pulse and heart rate) to better reflect the clinical concept of an actual pulse measurement.
